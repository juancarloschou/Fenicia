## Title
Fenicia — Merchant Kings: Mediterranean

## Subtitle
Turn-based Phoenician trading strategy. No timers. Pure commerce.

## Description
You are the founder of a Phoenician trading house around 800 BCE. Buy cheap, sail the Mediterranean, sell where demand is high, and build a dynasty that spans generations.

**Features:**
- Living economy — prices shift with supply and demand
- Turn-based — the world moves only when you press End Turn
- Discover ports — scout new markets or unlock them by sailing
- Pirates, events, and family traits across 5 generations
- Play in browser — mobile-friendly, no install needed

Inspired by Drug Wars, Patrician, and classic 4X turn pacing.
