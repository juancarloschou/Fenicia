# Roadmap — Post-Launch Features

## v1.0 — MVP (Current Design)

**Goal:** Playable on itch.io in browser. Prove the trade loop is fun.

- 6 ports, 6 goods, dynamic market
- 1–3 ships, 3 upgrade tiers
- Turn-based + Advance to Next Event
- Pirates (2 routes), 5 events
- Dynasty (5 generations, 8 traits, family relics)
- Map + port UI, save/load

**Ship when:** Core loop fun for 30+ minutes in internal playtest.

---

## v2 — Colonies & Exploration

**Goal:** Give mid/late-game something to spend gold on beyond ships.

### Colonies (Simplified)

- Found colony on empty coast (pay gold)
- Start: 20 inhabitants, produces wood
- **2 levels only** (not 5):
  - Level 1: Outpost (wood)
  - Level 2: Port (trade bonus + local storage)

### New Ports (3–4)

Discover via Explorer trait or gold investment:
- Sicily
- Sardinia
- Malta
- Southern Spain (Gadir)

### Builder Trait

Activates: −20% colony founding cost.

### New Gold Sinks

- Colony maintenance per turn
- Colony upgrade costs

---

## v3 — Technology & Nations

**Goal:** Long-term replayability and historical flavor.

### Technology Tree (6–8 nodes)

| Tech | Effect |
|------|--------|
| Square sail | +10% speed |
| Warehouses | +20% cargo |
| Accounting | −10% "tax" on trades |
| Shipyards | Unlock tier 4 ships |
| Agriculture | +regen on grain in colonies |
| Coinage | Markets regenerate faster |

One tech per generation or purchased with gold + time.

### Nations (Demand Modifiers)

AI doesn't trade — nations shift regional demand:

| Nation | Consumes |
|--------|----------|
| Egypt | Grain, pottery |
| Greece | Wine, dye |
| Assyria | Copper, wood |
| Carthage | (existing port, becomes nation hub) |

Events tied to nations: "Egypt declares embargo on dye" etc.

---

## v4 — Long Campaign Mode

**Goal:** Premium / "full experience" mode for engaged players.

- 10+ generations
- Historical narrative events:
  - Rise of Carthage
  - Greek colonization wave
  - Assyrian expansion
- Multiple victory conditions:
  - Wealth threshold
  - Trade volume
  - Colonies count
  - Prestige / influence
- Achievement / leaderboard on itch.io

---

## Explicitly Not Planned (Unless Demand)

- Real-time multiplayer trading
- Tactical naval combat
- Full 4X conquest
- Native mobile app store launch (stay web-first)
- NFT / blockchain anything
